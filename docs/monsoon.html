Why the obsession with July rainfall in India?

Examining drought from the 1870s to the present

The Indian monsoon is a period of heavy rainfall between June and September; it is brought about by multiple rain bearing winds that appraoch and retreat from different parts of the country at different times. Monsoon prediction keeps India's agro-based economy grounded, and each summer newspapers meticulosuly track daily rainfall. Why is it that stakes get higher come July each year?

This is why the media is so fixated on July rainfall. You generally know by the middle of the month if the monsson has failed.
