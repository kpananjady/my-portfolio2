Hi! My name is Kasturi Pananjady, and I am a data journalism master's student at Columbia University. I have previously worked at The Brown Daily Herald, The Wire and The Hindu. Those links should take you to the work I did there.

This is a website where I'm documenting some of the fabulous things I am learning in class. I often go back and revise my work when I feel like I can do a better job, so expect some changes! For instance, this iteration of my portfolio tinkers a lot with interactivity and responsiveness. In the further work section of my projects I often mull over what I'd like to do next. If you have feedback, please send it my way so I can add it to my list of things I need to learn!

P.S. I love pandas.
